About Us

Join with hundreds of Hotels and Resturants all over the city who are using Meal.ly to share more, care more and waste less. Through this platform, we are incubating hotels,resturants or other food organizations with NGOs to work for a cause for eradicating hunger issues by directly supplying food for those who are in need. Meal.ly bridges the gap between the one with ample amount of food and one who is short of it. We'll make it easier for the resturants to give away the excess food for the needy people in coordinaton with Non-profit organizations.
